DASS-21 Self-Assessment

The Depression, Anxiety and Stress Scale (DASS-21) is a clinically validated self-report tool developed at the University of New South Wales. It measures your emotional wellbeing across three dimensions with evidence-based severity ratings.

Important: This screening is not a substitute for professional diagnosis or treatment. If you're in crisis or experiencing thoughts of self-harm, please call iCall: 9152987821 or Vandrevala Foundation: 1860-2662-345 (24/7) immediately.
